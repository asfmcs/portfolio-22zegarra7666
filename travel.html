Hawaii, USA

It is recognized as one of it's states and it is made up of astonishing rugged landscapes, majestic waterfalls, tropical foliage and beautiful golden beaches. Animals

Hawaii is a beautiful place filled with plenty of unique Flora and Fauna, ranging from all sorts of animals and envioraments. There are many kinds of species that are exclusive to the enviorament and they all belong to different habitats including:

  1. The Hawaiian Monk Seal
  2. Wales and Dolphins
  3. The Hawaiian Hoary Bat
  4. The Indian Mongoose
  5. The Axis and Mule Deer
  6. The Feral Wallaby
  7. The Feral Cattle, Pig, Goat, Donkey, And Sheep
  8. Hawksbill Sea Turtle
  9. The Seabirds of Hawaii
  10. The Forest Birds of Hawaii
  11. And the Nene

The Nene is one of the most popular and recognizable Hawaiian animals. It is known to exclusively enhabit the Hawaiian islands of Maui, Oahu, Molokai and Hawai'i. The Nene is a geese specie that is believed to have evolved for over 500,000 years from the Canadian goose species. The Nene is about 41 cms tall, it is a herbivore that feeds off of seeds, fruits, flowers and leaves; and is believed to be one of the rarest kind of geese in the world!
